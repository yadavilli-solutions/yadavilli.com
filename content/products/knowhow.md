## From Knowledge to Action

**KnowHow** is where intelligence becomes operational. It takes the knowledge graphs built by DeepDive and your existing operational assets — incident logs, change records, monitoring data — and generates intelligent, context-aware runbooks that codify your organization's institutional knowledge.

### What KnowHow Does

- **Automated Runbook Generation**: Transform scattered operational knowledge into structured, versioned runbooks. KnowHow generates step-by-step procedures from your knowledge graph, incident history, and existing documentation.
- **Knowledge Graph Integration**: Directly consumes the knowledge graphs produced by **[DeepDive](/products/deepdive/)**, ensuring runbooks reflect the actual architecture, dependencies, and business logic of your systems.
- **Operational Playbooks**: Generate context-aware playbooks for common operational scenarios — deployments, incident response, scaling events, and maintenance windows — tailored to your specific infrastructure.
- **Incident Response Automation**: Build intelligent response procedures that incorporate system dependencies, blast radius analysis, and escalation paths — reducing mean time to resolution.

### How It Fits the Pipeline

KnowHow is the **Activate** phase of our **Calibrate · Decode · Activate** framework. After **[BenchMark](/products/benchmark/)** calibrates your landscape and **[DeepDive](/products/deepdive/)** decodes your system intelligence, KnowHow activates that knowledge into actionable operational excellence.
