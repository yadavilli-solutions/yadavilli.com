## The Challenge

A leading financial services provider was struggling with a massive library of legacy RPA bots. These bots were brittle, required constant maintenance, and couldn't handle even minor changes in UI or business logic, leading to frequent process failures.

## The Solution

We implemented a phased transition from RPA to **Agentic AI**:
1. **App Rationalization**: Used **[Benchmark](/products/benchmark)** to analyze the underlying legacy systems and identify core business processes handled by the RPA bots.
2. **Agent Design**: Built intelligent agents capable of reasoning through process exceptions and adapting to UI changes.
3. **PAaaS Deployment**: Deployed the new agents as a managed service (**Process Automation as a Service**), providing real-time monitoring and performance optimization.

## The Results

- **60% Reduction in Maintenance**: AI agents adapted to changes that previously broke RPA scripts.
- **Improved Reliability**: Process success rate increased from 82% to 99.5%.
- **Scalable Automation**: The new architecture allowed for the rapid deployment of new automation capabilities without increasing technical debt.
